Ladyface Alehouse & Brasserie

Ladyface Ale Companie is the Conejo Valley's first microbrewery. A warm, casual European-style brewpub, it features award-winning hand-crafted ales, including innovative, globally-inspired seasonal and barrel-aged releases. Ladyface Alehouse & Brasserie features its ales in many recipes with a menu rooted in traditional French and Belgian country dishes, seasonally inspired by California's bountiful local harvests. Founded by locals, Ladyface Ale Companie opened in the fall of 2009, finding its home at the foot of Ladyface Mountain.

Nicknamed the “Pink Hurricane”, B4T9 has grown into one of the country’s premier master’s women’s cycling teams. Competing in all disciplines from road to track to mountain, B4T9’s diverse team includes all levels of riders; from veteran elite racers to women who are entering the sport for the first time. Along with racing, B4T9 promotes the sport of cycling to girls and women through event sponsorship, fundraising, and camps and clinics that promote cycling safety and skills. B4T9’s goal is to get more women of all ages on a bike.
